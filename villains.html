Xenomorph

Alien


Xenomorphs are about two metres tall with a strong tail, sharp claws, elongated head with an inner jaw and acid for blood.

Ghost Face

Scream


Ghost Face has a simple costume consisting of a white rubber mask, a long black cloak with jagged edges, and a bloody eight inch hunting knife

The Predator

Predator 1987


The basis of a Predator's existence is to hunt other dangerous species for sport and honor. They stand at a height of about 7 feet and sport armor fashioned with the bones and hide of previous prey.

Leatherface

The Texas Chainsaw Massacre


Leatherface is a cannibal serial killer, known for wearing masks made of human skin. Hes been known to hunt down his prey with a chainsaw and mallet

Pinhead

Hellraiser


Pinhead is an extremely powerful being from hell. His preferred method of attack is to summon hooks and chains that mutilate victims, often tearing them apart.

Freddy Krueger

A Nightmare on Elm Street


Freddy is a dream-demon who attacks his victims from within their dreams. He is commonly identified by his burned, disfigured face, and trademark metal-clawed brown leather glove.

Chucky

Childs Play


Chucky is a vicious serial killer who transferred his soul into a popular kids toy.

Michael Myers

Halloween


Myers is characterized as a force of pure evil that cannot be stopped and possesses immortality due to his inhumanity.

Hannibal Lecter

The Silence of The Lambs


Lecter is a cannibal who loves that taset of human flesh. He is highly intelligent and cultured, with refined tastes and impeccable manners.
